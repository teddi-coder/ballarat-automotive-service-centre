Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to service my car?
We recommend servicing every 6 months or 10,000 km, whichever comes first. For vehicles under warranty, follow the manufacturer's logbook schedule.
What's included in a standard service?
Our BASC Standard Service includes oil and filter change, fluid level checks, tyre pressure adjustment, and a comprehensive safety inspection.
Will you service my car without voiding my warranty?
Yes. We follow the manufacturer's logbook schedule using recommended parts, keeping your warranty fully intact under Australian Consumer Law.
Do I need to book in advance?
Bookings are recommended, but feel free to call and we'll do our best to fit you in.
