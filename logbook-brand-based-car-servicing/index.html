Frequently Asked Questions

Will this void my warranty?
No. Under the Australian Consumer Law, having your vehicle serviced by a qualified independent mechanic using approved parts does not void your warranty. We stamp your logbook and follow the manufacturer's schedule.
What's included in a logbook service?
A logbook service covers everything specified by your vehicle manufacturer for the relevant service interval — typically oil and filter change, fluid checks and top-ups, brake inspection, tyre rotation and safety checks. Exact items vary by vehicle.
How often should I service my car?
Most manufacturers recommend service every 10,000 to 15,000 km or every 6 to 12 months — whichever comes first. Check your logbook for your specific vehicle's schedule.
Do you keep service records?
Yes. We maintain records of all services carried out. You'll also receive a written service report with each visit.
