Frequently Asked Questions

Will logbook servicing here void my warranty?

No. Under Australian Consumer Law, you can have your vehicle serviced by any licensed mechanic without voiding your manufacturer's warranty, provided the service meets the manufacturer's specifications and quality parts are used. We stamp your logbook and keep records.

What's included in a logbook service?

A logbook service follows your vehicle manufacturer's schedule, which typically includes oil and filter change, air filter, tyre rotation, fluid top-ups, brake inspection, and a safety check. The exact items depend on your vehicle's service interval.

How often should I service my car?

Most manufacturers recommend a service every 10,000–15,000 km or every 6–12 months, whichever comes first. Check your logbook for the exact interval for your make and model, or call us and we'll advise you.

Do you keep service records?

Yes. We document every service and stamp your logbook. We also keep records on file so you always have a history of work done on your vehicle.
