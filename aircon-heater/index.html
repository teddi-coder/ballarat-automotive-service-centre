Car Air Conditioning Regassing and Repairs

Over time, all car air conditioning systems lose refrigerant. A regas restores the refrigerant to manufacturer specifications, improving cooling performance significantly. Beyond regassing, we inspect the whole system to identify leaks, worn components or faults that could cause further issues.

What Our Air Conditioning Service Includes

  • System performance check before and after service
  • Pressure testing to identify leaks or underperformance
  • Refrigerant regas to manufacturer specifications
  • Inspection of compressor, condenser, evaporator and hoses
  • Heater core and blower motor assessment
  • Cabin filter check and replacement if needed

Whether your AC is blowing warm air, your heater is barely working, or you're getting unusual noises or smells, our team will diagnose and fix the issue properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my car AC not cold?
The most common cause is low refrigerant due to a slow leak. Other causes include a faulty compressor, a clogged condenser, or a failed expansion valve. A proper diagnostic check will identify the issue quickly.
What is regassing?
Regassing means removing the old refrigerant from the system and refilling it to the manufacturer's recommended level. It restores cooling performance and is typically needed every 2–3 years.
How long does it take?
A standard regas takes around 45–60 minutes. If additional diagnostics or repairs are needed, we'll let you know the timeframe before starting work.
Do you fix heaters too?
Yes. We service and repair the full climate control system including the heater core, blower motor, thermostat, and temperature controls.
