What we cover

Car Air Conditioning Regassing

Over time, air conditioning systems lose refrigerant, reducing their cooling efficiency. Our regassing service restores your system to manufacturer specification so you can enjoy a cool, comfortable cabin again.

Our AC service includes:

  • System performance check
  • Pressure testing
  • Refrigerant regas to manufacturer specifications
  • Inspection of key components including compressor, condenser and filter drier

Common Air Conditioning Issues We Fix

  • AC not cooling properly
  • Strange smells from vents
  • Unusual noises when AC is running
  • Heater not producing warm air
  • Intermittent cooling faults
