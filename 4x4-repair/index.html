Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service lifted or modified 4x4s?
Yes. We work on standard and modified 4x4s including vehicles with lift kits, aftermarket suspension, or additional accessories. Let us know about any modifications when you book.
Can you diagnose diesel issues?
Yes. We use modern diagnostic equipment to identify diesel faults including injector problems, EGR issues, DPF blockages, turbo faults and sensor failures.
Do you work on Hilux, Ranger, D-MAX?
Yes. We regularly service and repair all popular 4x4 utes including Toyota Hilux, Ford Ranger, Isuzu D-MAX, Mitsubishi Triton and more.
How often should a 4x4 be serviced?
Most manufacturers recommend servicing every 10,000–15,000 km or 6–12 months, whichever comes first. Working or towing vehicles may benefit from more frequent servicing. We'll advise based on your specific model and usage.
