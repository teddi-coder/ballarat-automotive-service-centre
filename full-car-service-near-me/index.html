Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a full service and a logbook service?
A full service covers all the major service items regardless of interval. A logbook service follows exactly what your manufacturer specifies for your vehicle's current mileage and age. We can carry out either or both.
How long does a full service take?
Most full car services take 1 to 2 hours depending on the vehicle and any additional items found during the inspection.
Will you check everything?
Yes. We inspect all the key safety and maintenance systems including brakes, tyres, steering, suspension, fluids and more — not just oil and filter.
How much does a full service cost?
Cost varies by vehicle type, service interval and any additional items required. We provide a clear quote before starting any work.
