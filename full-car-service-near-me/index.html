A full car service covers everything your vehicle needs to run safely and reliably. We go through your vehicle systematically — engine oil and filter, air filter, fluids, brakes, tyres, lights, belts, and a full safety inspection. Nothing is skipped, and nothing is added that isn't needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a full service and a logbook service?

A logbook service follows your manufacturer's specified schedule and is stamped in your logbook — it keeps your warranty valid. A full service is a comprehensive inspection and service of all key systems regardless of the manufacturer's schedule. We can advise which is right for your vehicle when you call.

How long does a full service take?

Most standard full services are completed within 2–4 hours. If we find something that needs additional attention, we'll call you to discuss before proceeding. Same-day pickup is usually possible for standard services.

Will you check everything?

Yes. A full service includes a comprehensive safety inspection covering brakes, tyres, lights, wipers, belts, hoses, fluids, steering, suspension and more. You'll receive a written report with the results.

How much does a full service cost?

Pricing depends on your vehicle's make, model, age and which service items are due. Call us with your vehicle details and we'll give you a clear quote upfront with no surprises.
