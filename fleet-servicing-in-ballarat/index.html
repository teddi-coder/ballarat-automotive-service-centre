Downtime Costs You

Reactive repairs are always more expensive and disruptive than planned maintenance. A well-managed servicing schedule prevents breakdowns, reduces unexpected costs and keeps your team on the road. We work with local businesses, trades and transport operators to set up service schedules that work around your operations.

Our fleet services include:

  • Scheduled logbook servicing for all fleet vehicles
  • Mechanical repairs and fault diagnosis
  • Roadworthy inspections and certificates
  • Brake, tyre and safety checks
  • Service records and reporting for your fleet manager
  • Flexible scheduling to minimise vehicle downtime

Frequently Asked Questions

How many vehicles can you manage?
We work with fleets ranging from a handful of utes through to larger commercial operations. Contact us to discuss your specific fleet size and requirements.
Do you work with fleet card providers?
Yes. We accept most major fleet cards. Contact us to confirm compatibility with your card provider.
Can you schedule around our business hours?
Yes. We can work with your schedule to minimise the time vehicles are off the road.
Do you provide service records?
Yes. We maintain detailed service records for all fleet vehicles. Records are available on request and useful for resale, compliance and insurance purposes.
