Frequently Asked Questions

Can you service my new Toyota without voiding the warranty?

Yes. Under Australian Consumer Law, having your Toyota serviced by a licensed independent mechanic using quality parts does not void your manufacturer's warranty. We stamp your logbook and keep full service records.

Do you service Hilux and LandCruiser diesel models?

Yes, diesel servicing is a core part of what we do. We service all diesel Toyota models including the Hilux, LandCruiser 70 and 200 Series, Prado and HiAce. We also carry out diesel diagnostics and repairs.

How often should my Toyota be serviced?

Most Toyota models require a service every 10,000–15,000 km or 6–12 months, whichever comes first. Some models — particularly diesels — have different intervals. Check your logbook or call us and we'll advise based on your model and driving conditions.

Do you use genuine Toyota parts?

We use quality OEM-equivalent parts that meet Toyota's specifications. In some cases genuine parts can be sourced on request. We'll always discuss parts options with you and confirm pricing before proceeding.
