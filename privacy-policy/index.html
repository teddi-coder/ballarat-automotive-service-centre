Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ballarat Automotive Service Centre ABN [TBC] ("we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, holds and discloses personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. What Personal Information We Collect

  • Name, email address and phone number
  • Vehicle make, model and year
  • Service requirements and information you provide
  • Website usage data via cookies and analytics tools

2. How We Collect Personal Information

We collect information when you submit a booking form, contact us by phone, SMS or email, or visit our premises.

3. Why We Collect Personal Information

  • To respond to booking enquiries and service requests
  • To provide automotive workshop services
  • To contact you regarding your appointment
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with our staff and third-party service providers (e.g. Google Analytics) under confidentiality obligations, or with government bodies where required by law.

5. Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss and unauthorised access.

6. Access and Correction

You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us at contact@ballaratservicecentre.com.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and Google Tag Manager to collect anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Complaints

Complaints may be directed to contact@ballaratservicecentre.com or to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at www.oaic.gov.au.
