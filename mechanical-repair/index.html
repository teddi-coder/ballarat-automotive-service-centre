Frequently Asked Questions

What mechanical repairs do you do?

We handle a wide range of repairs including engine diagnostics, brake repairs, suspension and steering, cooling system faults, electrical issues, exhaust, clutch and transmission repairs. If you're not sure whether we cover your specific issue, just call and we'll let you know.

Do you fix warning lights?

Yes. We use professional diagnostic scanning equipment to read fault codes and identify the root cause of warning lights. We'll explain what the code means in plain language and give you a clear quote before any repair work begins.

Can you diagnose the problem before I commit to repairs?

Absolutely. We always diagnose first and provide a written quote before any work starts. You're never committed to a repair until you've approved the cost. If the diagnostic reveals something minor, we'll tell you that too.

Do you offer a repair guarantee?

Yes. All mechanical repairs are covered by a 12-month / 20,000 km warranty on parts and labour (whichever comes first). If the same issue recurs within that period, bring it back and we'll assess and fix it at no charge.
